# Translator's Introduction

This edition is designed to present the Arabic text and the English rendering as a single reading experience rather than two disconnected layers. The aim is clarity without flattening the original texture of the source.

Where the Arabic carries idiom, compression, or rhetorical force that cannot be fully mirrored in English, the translation favors intelligibility first and leaves room for brief translator's notes where needed. Qur'anic and Hadith passages are grouped as paired Arabic and English blocks so the reader can move between the original and the translation without losing the structure of the citation.
